Private Tutoring

Personalized Lessons. Real Progress. Human Connection.

For 25 years, I've helped hundreds of students learn Portuguese through private tutoring — offering a personal, supportive, and flexible way to master the language at your own pace.

Whether you're a total beginner or already speaking some Portuguese, private lessons give you the individual attention, customized feedback, and real-life conversation practice that online courses simply can't match.

What's Included:

  • 4 private lessons per month (1x per week)
  • Live, 1-on-1 sessions with Isabel (via Zoom)
  • Tailored content based on your level, goals, and learning style
  • Homework, conversation exercises, and pronunciation tips
  • Investment: €300/month for 4 private sessions

Why Choose Private Tutoring over Self-Paced Courses?

While self-paced online courses are a great way to learn flexibly and independently, private tutoring is ideal if you want to:

  • ✅ Stay motivated with someone holding you accountable
  • ✅ Receive instant feedback on your pronunciation and grammar
  • ✅ Work on specific goals (e.g., passing exams, moving to Portugal, job interviews)
  • ✅ Ask questions in real time and avoid common misunderstandings
  • ✅ Practice real conversation with a native speaker
  • ✅ Build confidence through guided speaking practice
